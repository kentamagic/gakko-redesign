What is GAKKO?

Gakko is a summer camp dedicated to rethinking traditional approaches to education.

In an age where information is accessible to anyone with a web browser, the traditional roles of teachers and schools have hardly changed in the last hundred years. GAKKO was founded to explore ways to enrich relationships between teachers and students, to promote the arts in tandem with the sciences, and to find innovative ways to use technology in the classroom setting. In short, the goal of GAKKO is to shape the future of education.
